leechcraft by 0xd34df00d

Opensource network client providing a full-featured web browser, BitTorrent client, RSS/Atom reader and much more.

Dependencies

Qt 4.4.0 or higher. Boost 1.35 or higher (1.37 or higher is recommended) libtorrent-rasterbar from trunk or own repos for the BitTorrent plugin.

Install

Up-to-date instructions are at http://deviant-soft.ws/leechcraft-building
